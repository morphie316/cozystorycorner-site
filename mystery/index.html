How to Play

🎲 Roll 1d6. A 4, 5, or 6 succeeds.

⭐ Each player starts with 3 hero tokens.

💡 If a roll fails, spend 1 token to still succeed.

🔁 No token? Try another way with a different skill, or let a friendly NPC help.

🧒 Suggested skills: Strong, Fast, Clever, Kind, Magic.
